Remember the Rodeo

Folks, bring the family and kids out for a night of fun this July. Returning this year is the long-time favorite "The Famous Preston Night Rodeo". This year's show will be held on July 29, 30, and 31. Tickets go on sale on May 20, 2021.
As a special treat this year, Garth Brooks will be returning to take on the most sinister bull this side of the Mississippi, Lord Voldermort. Fred and George Weasley, our famously hilarious clowns, will also be returning. The Weasley Clowns cannot wait to provide good family entertainment, and of course, to assist the competitors if needed. Elvis Presley will be the opening night's headliner.
