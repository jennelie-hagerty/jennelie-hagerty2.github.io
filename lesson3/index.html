Purpose

The purpose of the Weather 2 Go website is to provide the general public with weather condictions for the area of Southeast Idaho. The specific areas covered are Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The general public can also find local stories, local events, and a gallery containing photos of unique and awesome weather.
