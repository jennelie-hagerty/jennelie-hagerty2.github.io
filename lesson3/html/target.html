Target Audience

The audience for this site are people from all walks of life. They will be looking for the current weather condictions and also the extended forecast for the areas of Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and surrounding areas. They will use this information to plan their day trips, vacations, and their work day. The audience will also use this site to catch up on the local news and to be informed of new and reoccuring events in their area.

Persona 1

Marena is a busy mom of five children. Besides juggling her childern's busy schedules, she also keeps up with her husband's busy travel schedule. On the weekends and days off from school Marena likes to plan trips for her children so that they may go exploring their state. Also her husband has to travel for his job and the National Guard.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be for school?
  • What is the weather going to be for the drive to and from work?
  • What is the weather for this weekend for a trip the Lava Hot Springs?
  • Are there any historical events happening this weekend?

Persona 2

Bryan is a business man who lives in New York. He travels often for his job, usually to Southeast Idaho. Bryan has fallen in love with the area and is bringing his family on vacation to Fish Haven to enjoy the lake and surrounding area. Bryan uses weather2go.net when he is in the area to check the weather and to find interesting events to attend (if his work schedule allows).

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be for the week of vacation?
  • Is the weather suitable for boating?
  • What are some local events?
  • Is there any news that might effect our trip?
